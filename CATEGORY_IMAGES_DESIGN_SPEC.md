# Category Images Design Specification

## Project Overview
We need category illustration images for the AfriGos marketplace homepage. These images will be displayed as clickable cards in the "Shop by Category" section, showcasing different product categories with visually appealing, category-specific imagery.

---

## Technical Specifications

### Image Dimensions
- **Primary Size**: 1200px × 900px (4:3 aspect ratio)
- **Minimum Size**: 800px × 600px (4:3 aspect ratio)
- **File Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (under 300KB per image recommended)
- **Color Mode**: RGB
- **Resolution**: 72-150 DPI (web standard)

### Aspect Ratio
- **Strict Requirement**: 4:3 (width:height)
- All images MUST maintain this ratio
- Images will be cropped to fit if ratio is incorrect

---

## Display Context

### Grid Layout
- **Mobile devices**: 2 columns (each card is ~50% width)
- **Tablet/Desktop**: 4 columns (each card is ~25% width)
- **Container padding**: 16px on mobile, 24px on tablet/desktop
- **Gap between cards**: 16px on mobile, 24px on tablet/desktop

### Visual Treatment on Website
- Images will have a **dark gradient overlay** at the bottom (60% opacity black)
- Category name will be displayed in **white text** at the bottom left
- Product count badge may appear in bottom right
- Hover effect: Image scales up to 110% (slight zoom)
- Border radius: Rounded corners (8px)

### Safe Zone
- Keep important visual elements within the **center 80%** of the image
- **Bottom 30%** will have text overlay - avoid placing important details here
- **Top 20%** is always visible - good for main subject placement

---

## Category List & Image Requirements

Please create category-specific images that clearly illustrate each category. Images should be:

1. **Visually distinctive** - Instantly recognizable as the category
2. **Appealing to African marketplace context** - Products/services relevant to African consumers
3. **Professional quality** - Clean, well-composed, high-quality imagery
4. **Brand consistent** - Maintain a cohesive visual style across all category images

### Categories to Create (with suggested themes):

1. **Food & Beverages**
   - Illustration: African cuisine, fresh produce, traditional dishes, beverages
   - Colors: Warm tones (oranges, reds, greens)
   - Mood: Fresh, appetizing, authentic

2. **Fashion & Clothing**
   - Illustration: African textiles, modern clothing, accessories, traditional wear
   - Colors: Vibrant colors, patterns (Ankara, Kente inspired)
   - Mood: Stylish, vibrant, contemporary

3. **Beauty & Personal Care**
   - Illustration: Skincare products, haircare, cosmetics, wellness items
   - Colors: Soft pastels, pinks, purples
   - Mood: Fresh, clean, luxurious

4. **Health & Wellness**
   - Illustration: Vitamins, supplements, fitness equipment, health products
   - Colors: Green, blue, white
   - Mood: Healthy, clean, trustworthy

5. **Home & Garden**
   - Illustration: Home decor, furniture, garden tools, household items
   - Colors: Earth tones, browns, greens
   - Mood: Comfortable, welcoming, organized

6. **Electronics**
   - Illustration: Smartphones, gadgets, accessories, tech products
   - Colors: Modern blues, blacks, metallics
   - Mood: Modern, sleek, innovative

7. **Sports & Outdoors**
   - Illustration: Sports equipment, outdoor gear, fitness items
   - Colors: Energetic colors (reds, blues, greens)
   - Mood: Active, dynamic, adventurous

8. **Books & Media**
   - Illustration: Books, educational materials, media products
   - Colors: Warm neutrals, blues
   - Mood: Intellectual, organized, inviting

## Design Guidelines

### Style Recommendations
- **Photography preferred** but illustrations are acceptable
- **High-quality product photography** or lifestyle images
- Consistent lighting and color treatment across all images
- Professional, market-ready appearance
- Avoid cluttered compositions - keep it clean and focused

### Color Palette Considerations
- Images should work well with **red/primary brand colors** (overlay will be black)
- Ensure good contrast for white text overlay at bottom
- Consider that a dark gradient will be applied at the bottom

### Composition Tips
- Use **rule of thirds** for visual interest
- Ensure main subject is clear and recognizable
- Leave space at bottom for text overlay (bottom 30%)
- Consider how images look at different sizes (thumbnail to full width)

---

## File Naming Convention

Please name files using this format:
- Use category name in lowercase
- Replace spaces with hyphens
- Remove special characters
- Add file extension

**Examples:**
- `food-beverages.jpg`
- `fashion-clothing.jpg`
- `beauty-personal-care.jpg`
- `health-wellness.jpg`
- `home-garden.jpg`
- `electronics.jpg`
- `sports-outdoors.jpg`
- `books-media.jpg`
